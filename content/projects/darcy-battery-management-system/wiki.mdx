# Darcy Battery Management System

## Purpose

The Darcy Battery Management System project documents a power and battery-management PCB package from exported Altium schematic artifacts. The available PDF contains 10 pages covering top level, primary sheet, power, load switching, ADC, connector load switches, and current-loop circuitry.

## Requirements

- Preserve the exported schematic PDF as the source artifact.
- Add board layout renders when Gerber-derived or manually exported SVGs are available.
- Add the Altium STEP export for the interactive 3D model when available.
- Keep the project static-first and avoid raw Altium document parsing.

## System Context

The board belongs in the power electronics and avionics portion of the Darcy stack. The portfolio focus should be power-path decisions, load-switch channel consistency, measurement strategy, and validation planning.

## Architecture

The schematic package includes a top-level view, a primary sheet, a power sheet, a load-switch sheet, ADC measurement, multiple connector load-switch sheets, and a current-loop sheet. That organization suggests a design centered on controlled power distribution and monitored outputs.
